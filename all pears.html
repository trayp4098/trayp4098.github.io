Agridera's Fiery Harvest sweet Pear Varieties

×××××

At Agridera, our mission is to revolutionize the pear industry by producing top-tier pear varieties tailored to meet the demands of growers and consumers worldwide. Through relentless research and innovation, we strive to offer solutions that elevate agricultural practices and delight taste buds across the globe.

Our primary focus lies in developing pear cultivars with robust resistance to prevalent diseases like fire blight and pear scab. We're dedicated to creating varieties that thrive in diverse climates and conditions, ensuring a bountiful harvest for farmers everywhere.

In addition to disease resistance, we prioritize horticultural traits such as early maturation, varied pear sizes, firm texture, appealing skin color (e.g., green, yellow, red blush), sweet and aromatic flavor, resistance to cracking and bruising, extended storage life, and adaptability to both warm and cool climates. Our portfolio encompasses a range of pear shapes, from classic pyriform to round and bell-shaped, each boasting a unique combination of quality attributes and delectable taste.

At Agridera, our dedication to excellence extends beyond tomatoes to encompass the delightful world of pears. We've strategically structured our breeding programs around three key categories: Orchard Grown Early Season Pears, Orchard Grown Main Season Pears, and Compact Tree Open-field Pears.

Greenhouse Pear Varieties
Cultivation Strategy

Our greenhouse-focused breeding program targets the discerning markets of Northern Europe and Canada. We prioritize the production of both single and clustered fruit varieties, boasting classic pear shapes and fortified against a spectrum of threats including Pear Psylla, Pear Scab, and Phytophthora root rot. Some of our hybrids are engineered with additional safeguards, such as resistance to powdery mildew and fire blight.

Orchard Grown Indeterminate
Pear Varieties

Tailored for the vibrant markets of Argentina, South Africa, and Italy. Our open-field breeding endeavors are dedicated to cultivating large, classic-shaped fruits weighing over 180 grams. These varieties are engineered to withstand the onslaught of Pear Psylla, Pear Scab, and Phytophthora root rot. In our continuous pursuit of excellence, future iterations will also integrate resistance against fire blight as a foundational trait in our cultivars.

Compact Tree Open-field
Pear Varieties

Our compact tree open-field pear breeding program caters to the tastes of the Mediterranean and Eastern European markets. We meticulously craft varieties in both classic and round shapes, available in standard sizes ranging from 120 to 160 grams, as well as larger sizes exceeding 160 grams, up to 250 grams and beyond. These hybrids are fortified with core resistances against Pear Psylla, Pear Scab, and Phytophthora root rot. For added protection, select hybrids are fortified with additional defenses, including resilience against powdery mildew and fire blight.

Agridera collaborates with a network of international and local distributors spanning the globe.
